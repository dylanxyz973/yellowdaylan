I created the following which are part of the requirements of this project demonstrating creativity and technical skill:

• Name Card (Front and Back)
• Four tabs of Webpages (Home, About, Portfolio, Contact)
• A set of Five Stickers

Technical Competencies:

Building process:

Skills needed would be originality, creativity, design consistency and human interest in the design composition and concept development. We are supposed to choose a theme based on what your ideal business is supposed to be. The theme of my design is fashion retail business because I enjoy fashion a lot, I decided to create a theme related to fashion which shows my ideal business as a fashion designer. The business name card is designed with fashion elements in mind and stickers which are related to possible items which are sold in fashion retail stores.
