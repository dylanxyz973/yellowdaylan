Game Design Document 📄 Ideal User Profile 📄

Technical Competencies


Unity Engine (3D & VR Development)

• Usage of Unity platform to build a functional 3D escape room environment which includes room layout, walls, ceilings and object placement.

Prefab Management & Asset and Audio Integration

• Imported and configured free prefabs from the Unity Asset Store.
• Troubleshoot and adjusting of prefabs when did not function as intended.
• Imported and integrated audio assets into prefabs and interactive objects to enhance gameplay feedback.

C# Scripting

Implemented basic C# scripts to support puzzle logic, object interactions and in-game response required for escape room puzzles to be functional.

Scene Optimisation & Performance Handling

Managed long loading times and performance issues by chossing assets that are low in size and handling crashes during development.

User Interaction & VR Usability Design

Designed VR interactions suitable for first-time VR users focusing on accessiblity, navigation, intuitive object interaction, and a single-room layout to reduce motion discomfort.

Problem Solving & Debugging

Identified and resolved development issues such as broken prefabs, unexpected behaviour, adjusting components, colliders and scripts accordingly and correcting game build settings.

Project Management

Demonstrated planning and time management skills by organising development tasks and maintaining consistent progress throughout the project duration.

Creative Game Design Decisions

Developed original ideas by deviating from typical horror-escape rooms while focusing on a brighter style concept with a more accessible experience while retaining core escape room mechanics.

Features

Gameplay Mechanics

• Puzzles requires logical reasoning and observation
• Sequential puzzle progression (solving one puzzle unlocks the next)
• A variety of puzzles types such as object arrangement/sorting and finding correct answer
• Hidden clues integrated naturally within the environment
• Interactive 3D objects that players can grab, inspect and reposition or trigger puzzle-related events

In-Game Experience

• Clear written instructions outlining the steps required to solve each puzzle
• Room-scale exploration within a single enclosed space
• Straightforward VR controls designed for beginners
• Comfortable navigation layout to reduce motion discomfort

Immersive Sound

• Ambient background audio to enhance atmosphere
• Sound feedback when interacting with interactable objects
• Audio cues when puzzles are successfully completed

Design Theme

• Bright, non-horror themed escape room concept
• Single-room layout focused on accessibility and ease of movement
• Intuitive puzzle design to prevent confusing players
• Logical clue placement encouraging observation and critical thinking

Building process

This escape room is created with the timeline of 8 weeks averaging of 3 hours spent daily. I created the room walls and ceiling from unity building materials and the prefabs/objects are imported from the unity asset store. I faced multiple challenges building the escape room such as prefab not working as intended, long loading times and multiple computer crashes. Sadly, due to unforeseen circumstances, one of the planned puzzles could not be completed as originally intended.

Usually, escape rooms are built in a horror and creepy way with multiple rooms but I decided to create something that is original and refreshing making the game more unique and I made mine a single room because it is much more user-friendly to first timer VR users due to easy movement and navigation around the room.
