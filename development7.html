Objectives

The objective of this project is to design and develop a creative personal portfolio website that showcases my projects, skills, and technical competencies. The portfolio highlights my front-end development abilities while applying modern UI/UX principles with gradient styling and interactive elements.

Building process

This portfolio was developed as part of a school assignment to strengthen my understanding of front-end web development fundamentals through practical implementation.

Creative gradient styling was applied to enhance visual appeal and make the portfolio stand out. While gradients improve aesthetics, careful attention was required to ensure text readability and layout balance. During development, I encountered positioning challenges such as maintaining consistent placement of navigation elements when images resized. This required adjustments in CSS positioning, container structure, and responsiveness handling.

Through debugging and refining layout structure, I improved my understanding of CSS positioning, layering (z-index), and responsive design techniques.
