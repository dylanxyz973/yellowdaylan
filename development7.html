Building process

This portfolio was developed across a period of 24 weeks (two semesters) with the goal of establishing a personal identity as either a designer or an application developer.

The first 12 weeks (first semester) focused mainly on experimentation with different various schemes and idea generation while conceptualisng feasible ideas. During this phase, I explored various layout possibilities and design styles while also developing a stronger understanding of design vocabulary and visual composition.

The next 12 weeks (second semester) involved the website development under the supervision of a portfolio supervisor. With some guidance, I was able to refine the concept and gain a clearer understanding of what content is appropriate for a professional portfolio that will be used for internship or job interviews.

Throughout the development process, I had several misunderstandings of a sytlish design concept and executing my concept was rather challeniging as I opted for an unconventional styling and layout. Multicoloured gradient background images was preferred instead of multicoloured CSS gradients to maintain consistent colour harmony and visual quality across different pages

Why did I chose to apply a multicoloured gradient styling as I feel it gives a personality of being vibrant and energetic yet modernised which is importang key consideration during development was balancing visual creativity with usability. While the gradient theme helped create a vibrant visual identity, careful adjustments were needed to ensure that text remained readable and that important content remained clear to users. Meticulous attention was given to ensure text readability and finding a suitable font colour was a main concern. Page Navigation and interactivity such as filters, image galleries, and toggle sections were important features that I was part of the execution which allows users to explore the portfolio content in a well meaningful and engaging manner. During development, I encountered multiple positioning challenges such as wrong placement of elements which is because of multiple CSS layout techniques.

Overall, the development process helped me strengthen both my technical implementation skills and my understanding of design decisions required when building a creative professional portfolio website.
